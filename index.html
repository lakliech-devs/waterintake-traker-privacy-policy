Privacy Policy for WaterIntake Tracker

Last Updated: March 11, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to WaterIntake Tracker! This privacy policy explains how our application handles your data and protects your privacy. We are committed to ensuring the confidentiality and security of your information.

Summary

Water Tracker respects your privacy:

  • All your data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on external servers
  • Your water intake data never leaves your device
  • We use minimal permissions, only what's needed for the app to function

Information Collection and Use

Local Storage

All information you enter in the Water Tracker app (including water intake records, daily goals, and reminder preferences) is stored entirely on your device using local storage mechanisms (SharedPreferences). This data does not leave your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Types of Data Stored Locally

  • Water intake records (amount and timestamp)
  • Daily water intake goals
  • Notification/reminder preferences
  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)

Advertisements

Our app contains advertisements provided through Google Mobile Ads. While using these services:

  • The ad provider may collect certain information as outlined in their privacy policy
  • We do not share your water tracking data with advertisers
  • Advertisers may use device identifiers to serve relevant ads

For more information about Google's ad policies and data collection, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Permissions

Notifications

If you enable reminders, our app will request permission to send you notifications. These notifications are scheduled and triggered locally on your device and do not involve any external servers.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can:

  • Clear app data through your device's settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all associated data

Children's Privacy

Our application is designed for general use and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
